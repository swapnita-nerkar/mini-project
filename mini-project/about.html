Indian food is different from rest of the world not only in taste but also in
cooking methods.It reflects a perfect blend of various cultures and ages.
Just like Indian culture, food in India has also been influenced
by various civilizations, which have contributed their share in its
overall development and the present form.

Foods of India are better known for its spiciness. Throughout India,
be it North India or South India, spices are used generously in food.
But one must not forget that every single spice used in Indian
dishes carries some or the other nutritional as well as medicinal properties.
